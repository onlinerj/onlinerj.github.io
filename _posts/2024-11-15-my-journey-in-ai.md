## The Beginning

My journey in AI started during my Bachelor's at IIT (ISM) Dhanbad, where I studied Computer Science and Engineering. The foundation in data structures, algorithms, and distributed systems would prove invaluable in my future work.

## Industry Experience

### Early Career at Halliburton (2019-2023)

I started as an Associate Software Engineer and Data Scientist at Halliburton's Big Data Centre of Excellence. Working with large-scale data systems across Middle East Asia, North Africa, India, and SEA gave me hands-on experience with real-world AI applications.

### Entrepreneurship (2023-2024)

Co-founded Webzite Edge Technologies as CTO, which was later acquired. This experience taught me about building AI products from the ground up and leading technical teams.

### Leading GenAI at Truxt.AI (2024)

As Generative AI Lead, I've been working on cutting-edge LLM applications, focusing on privacy, security, and practical deployment challenges.

## Academic Pursuit

### Master's in AI at Stevens Institute of Technology

I decided to pursue an MS in Artificial Intelligence to deepen my research capabilities. The challenge? Complete a 2-year program in 11 months while maintaining a perfect 4.0 GPA and conducting publishable research.

**Key courses:**
- Deep Learning
- Applied Machine Learning
- Accelerated Computing on GPU Clusters and SuperComputers
- Big Data Analytics
- Special Problems in Artificial Intelligence
