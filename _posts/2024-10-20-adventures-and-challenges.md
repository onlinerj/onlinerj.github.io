Beyond AI and technology, I'm passionate about pushing my limits through travel and personal challenges.

## Epic Adventures

### Mount Everest Base Camp

Reaching the base camp of the world's highest peak was a humbling experience. The majestic Himalayas taught me about perseverance and the beauty of nature.

### Chadar Trek

One of my most challenging adventures: trekking 50km on a frozen river in the middle of nowhere at **-40°C** and 4000 metre altitude with a 20kg backpack. This trek tested my physical and mental limits.

### Highest Bungee Jump

Conquered the highest bungee jump in the world - an adrenaline rush like no other!

### Astrophotography

Shot a time lapse of the Milky Way Galaxy from the only Dark Sky reserve in India on a new Moon Day. The universe is truly magnificent when you can see it clearly.

## Fitness Challenges

### 7.5 Day Fast

Completed a 7.5-day fast on just salt and water. This was as much a mental challenge as a physical one, teaching me about discipline and self-control.

### MyFitnessPal Streak

Maintained a **1000 days streak** logging calorie intake consistently. Small daily habits compound into significant results.
